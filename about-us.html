Why us

We are a small diverse company established in July 2016 since then we have ventured into Cloud Based software, Data Cabling and providing Skilled Resource.

IT Kiwi Consultants Limited was the brain child of Niran Pieries. Niran has a wealth of knowledge in the Information Technology industry working in New Zealand for the past 25 years.

Our operation centre is in Wellington even though some of our staff works at customer sites around the country.

Our passion is to build relationships with our customers engaging in business for the long-term benefit.

Our Cloud Based products are geared to improve your business bringing greater revenue, having a proven track record in one of the biggest markets in the world.

“Building Relationships” is the most important part of our business. We ensure our resources are skilled and smart, our services are value added and helps to grow our customers businesses along with ours.
