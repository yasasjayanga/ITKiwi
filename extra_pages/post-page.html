Iphone repair techniques

02/07/17
3

A lot of fuss has been made about the iPhone’s lack of repairability ever since it debuted in 2007 without a removeable battery. Six years of design updates later and the iPhone is still as hard to tinker with as ever, unless of course you work at Apple.

Along with detailed tips on the new iPhone components, the exclusive shots below show the exact methods Apple staff use to repair broken iPhones, thanks to an array of tools created to make the process more efficient, including a new Universal Display Removal Fixture and an iPhone Battery Fixture Apple keeps hidden in the back of Apple Stores. The most important principles to remember when setting out to repair an iPhone are:

  • Use Universal display Removal Fixture to open the device
  • Remove the the battery through prying off its adhesive but be patient
  • Lift the battery out of the case using a black stick
  • Before instaliing a new battery, replace the old adhesive with a new one
  • You can install a speaker module by fitting it instead of the old one

These are some of the most useful but certainly not all iPhone repair techniques that our experts use to make your Apple device work just as before.
